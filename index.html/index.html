About Raven

Raven is a fictional character in the Teen Titans comic book series and its animated adaptations. She is a superhero with magical powers, including telekinesis, levitation, and the ability to project dark energy. She is often depicted as a serious and reserved character who struggles to control her emotions, which are linked to her powers. Raven is also known for her iconic dark cloak and hooded appearance, which is meant to symbolize her isolation and her struggle to control her dark side.
